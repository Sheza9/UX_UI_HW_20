Problem Statement:

We believe that prioritizing health and fitness tracking for dog owners seeking an active lifestyle for their pets and them will achieve a happier and healthier lifestyle. Users are struggling to find trails and walks that meet their and their dogs personalized needs. Our app is designed to support individuals in tracking their fitness with their furry friends and find walks and trails that have the individualized dog amenities they are looking for. We have observed in user interviews that there is a need in the market for a product that will promote fitness accountability and socialization with pets, friends, and family alike. By combining personalized walks that meet activity goals and addressing dog and owner needs, we will create a better daily walking experience for pet owners.
